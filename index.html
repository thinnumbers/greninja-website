GRENINJA
Greninja is a
Water
and
Dark
type ninja frog Pokemon. It moves with speed and grace like a ninja. It can form water shurikens using compressed water to slice through opponents. These throwing stars are sharp enough to split metal.
Greninja has red eyes with white pupils and its mouth is hidden behind a large, pink tongue that wraps around its neck and extends outward behind its head. It can also appear and disappear using a technique called
Shadow Sneak
