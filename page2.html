FROAKIE
Froakie is a
Water
type bubble frog Pokemon. It can produce a large mane of bubbles from its chest and back and has two small bubbles on its nose. The delicate yet flexible bubbles, called Frubbles, reduce the damage Froakie takes from attacks.
Froakie is one of the three starters offered from the Kalos region. The other starters are Chespin, the
Grass
type, and Fennekin, the
Fire
type. These Pokemon are given by Professor Sycamore if any new Pokemon trainer happens to start their journey in the Kalos region. Froakie will evolve to Frogadier at level 16. Click below to learn more!
